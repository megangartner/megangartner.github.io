Goat Cheese and Shallot Dip

About this Recipe

This recipe is a great appetizer for get togethers. Usually we serve it on slices of toasted baguette and sprinkle the crushed peppercorn on top.

Ingredients

  1. 5 oz. soft goat cheese, at room temperature
  2. 4 to 6 Tbs. heavy cream
  3. 1/2 tsp salt
  4. 2 or 3 small shallots, minced
  5. 3 Tbs. pink peppercorns, smashed

Directions

Put the goat cheese in a bowl and mash it with a fork.
Add 1 Tbs. of the cream and mash it in. Continue to add the cream, 1 Tbs. at a time, until you have a soft, mild spread.
Mix in the salt and the shallots to taste.
When ready to serve, spread the cheese and shallot spread on any type of cracker or toast and top with several pink peppercorns.
