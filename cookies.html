Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

About this Recipe

These are your basic chocolate chip cookie recipe. You can make it your own with any additions or substitutes. I like to use a mini ice cream scoop to shape the dough, but obviously by hand will work. These will be more of a soft and chewey type of cookies rather than crispy.

Ingredients

  1. 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  2. 1 teaspoon baking soda
  3. 1 teaspoon salt
  4. 1 cup (2 sticks) butter or margarine, softened
  5. 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  6. 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  7. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  8. 2 large eggs
  9. 1 3/4 cups (11.5-oz. pkg.)
  10. 1 cup chopped nuts

Directions

PREHEAT oven to 375Â° F.COMBINE flour, baking soda and salt in small bowl. Beat butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar and vanilla extract in large mixer bowl until creamy. Add eggs one at a time, beating after each addition. Gradually beat in dry ingredients. Stir in chocolate chips and any other additions. Drop by tablespoon onto ungreased baking sheets.BAKE for 9 to 11 minutes or until golden brown.
